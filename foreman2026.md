## Foreman + OpenBolt - a <3 story in three acts

Abstract:

End of 2024, we started a journey of [integrating OpenBolt into Foreman](https://community.theforeman.org/t/rfc-integrating-bolt-into-remote-execution/39901).
This turned into a series of conference talks:

At the Foreman Birthday Party 2025 I gave a presentation about migrating from [Puppet Enterprise to Foreman + OpenVox](https://bastelfreak.de/foreman2025/index.html#1).
At the Config Management Camp 2026 I spoke about [integrating OpenBolt tasks into Foreman](https://bastelfreak.de/cfgmgmtcamp2026/openbolt.html#1).
This talk is part three of the series - integrating [Choria](https://choria.io/) as a transport protocol for scaleable remote execution and orchestration with the [OpenBolt Plugin](https://github.com/overlookinfra/foreman_openbolt).

We will go into the details on:

* What actually is Choria?
* How do I configure this?
* Why do I want to use this awesome OpenBolt integration for all of my daily sysops tasks and platform orchestration?
* Why is this the perfect combination to Foreman and OpenVox and the missing piece for orchestration (why is it cooler than Ansible or the existing remote execution in Foreman)?
